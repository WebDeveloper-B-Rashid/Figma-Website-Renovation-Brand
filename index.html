Why us?

Craftsmen You Can Trust

Since 2012, Nobel Basement Builders has served as the premier legal basement suite development company in Calgary and the surrounding area. With a sole focus on basements, we provide exceptional experience in converting unused areas into useful, livable suites. A smooth permitting process is ensured by our extensive expertise in local building standards and regulations. We take care of everything, from the first design to the last inspection, assisting you in utilizing Calgary's rezoning chances to build basement suites that generate revenue and adhere to the highest standards.
